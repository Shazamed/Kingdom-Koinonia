Beliefs

Beliefs of Kingdom Koinonia

  1. First, we believe we are primarily here for God the Father, God the Son and God the Spirit – The Holy Trinity. We seek to have a deep spiritual relationship with The Holy Trinity.
  2. Second, we are to genuinely Love Thy Neighbour as Thy Self without strings attached.
  3. Third, we must return to living by the Spirit and allow the Head of the Church – Jesus Christ, to take the reins of building His Church. His Church is not defined by a building or the works of men, but by the Holy Spirit whose indwelling is in each person’s life.
  4. Fourth, we must reinforce discipleship. Inspire everyone to seek the fruit of the Spirit, integrity, humility, generosity and holiness. All these are rooted in the Word of God – the Bible and anchored on the Hope of His promised return.
  5. Fifth, God has and is raising up men/women with a true father’s heart who will be voices of wisdom, and not echoes to this generation. These spiritual parents will not be controlling but they will be affirming.
  6. Sixth, we need to put away our agenda and preconceived notions of what must be done and when. We need to follow God’s will, agenda, listen more, and talk less.
  7. Seventh, we must recognise and be prepared that where the Lord is taking us “we have not walked this way before.” He will be the pillar of cloud by day and fire by night. We need to trust and have faith in Him.
