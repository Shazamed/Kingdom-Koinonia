Features

Key features of a Microchurch

  • Small community (not more than 30)
  • Low cost (no heavy overheads cost to pay salary, building infrastructure etc)
  • Simple (able to express herself in different ways, different contexts, and different cultures)
  • Empowerment (spiritual authority and autonomy to implement ideas, gives leaders passion and ownership, which is powerful and self-sustaining)
  • Replicable (easy to start, lead and contextualized)

Why are these features important?

  • Small is More (more accessible, more connected, more relational)
  • Minimal cost (more funds are channeled into ministry outcomes)
  • Liberating (removing size and not thinking in terms of numbers opened up the possibility for more contextualized church to form)
  • Free (able to express herself and accommodate in different ways, different contexts, and different cultures)
  • Autonomy (free to be creative, flexible, contextualize)
  • Flexible (nimble enough to change/reproduce quickly to affect those contexts)

If we want to see the Kingdom grow beyond our control, we must release control.
